    Corporate ethics

    Last modification: 26 October 2023

    WISY LTD, a company, registered in the Suite 3, 1st Floor, La Ciotat Building, Mont Fleuri, Mahe, Seychelles under laws of 238205 ("WISY", "we", “us”, “our”) and you, who sign up for and access our web services, (“User”, “you") agree to conclude this Corporate ethics (“Ethics”). This Policy applies to https://wisymoney.com (“Site”) and all apps owned and operated by WISY (“Apps”).

    1. General provisions Copy

    1.1. Within WISY, corporate ethics form an intricate framework of standards, principles, and values that govern the conduct and positioning of Site Users, influencers, and other participants as they engage with the Site and Apps ("Services").

    1.2. These corporate ethics encompass pillars such as social responsibility, unwavering honesty, impartial justice, genuine respect for fellow individuals, and a steadfast commitment to upholding human rights.

    1.3. The significance of these corporate ethics cannot be overstated, as they play a pivotal role in shaping a positive perception of WISY. Furthermore, they are instrumental in nurturing robust relationships with clientele, partners, and the workforce, all while fostering the sustainable growth of the business.

    2. Basic Principles of Corporate Ethics Copy

    2.1. Company values and principles:

    Upholding respect for Users, partners, the project team, and the community;

    Establishing a secure and welcoming environment for Users;

    Demonstrating transparency and honesty in all facets of our operations;

    Prioritizing innovation and quality in the advancement of our Services;

    Taking responsibility for the actions and outcomes linked to brand promotion.

    2.2. Permitted means of promotion:

    Collaborating with influencers who align with WISY's corporate values and have expertise in online gaming or the cryptocurrency sector.

    Engaging in blockchain events, conferences, summits, and exhibitions.

    Releasing articles and video reviews that showcase the benefits and innovations of WISY's Services.

    Generating high-quality and informative content for social networks and platforms associated with the blockchain industry.

    2.3. Unacceptable methods for promoting WISY include:

    Employing spam or intrusive advertising.

    Exploiting the vulnerabilities of Users, Partners, the Project Team, and the Community for personal gain.

    Utilizing insider information to enrich oneself.

    Presenting WISY in a negative light when compared to its competitors.

    Focusing on accumulating investments with the intention of achieving quick and effortless profits.

    Dissemination of information that may tarnish the reputation of WISY.

    Revealing confidential information about Users.

    Encouraging the use of WISY for credit or loan funds.

    Distorting information about WISY, its Services, or the project team, and engaging in falsehoods and defamation.

    Avoiding the use of terms such as "earnings," "income," "passive income," "deposit," and "investment”.

    3. Responsibilities and control Copy

    3.1. WISY monitors the compliance of influencers with Corporate Ethics, Terms of Use and platforms on which content is published.

    3.2. In the event of breaches of the Corporate Ethics, Terms of Use and platforms on which content is published, WISY shall take measures to eliminate them and prevent the recurrence of such situations.

    4. Updating the terms of Corporate ethics Copy

    4.1. WISY reserves the right to periodically review and update Corporate Ethics, considering changes in legislation, industry standards, as well as experience and feedback from influencers and the community.

    4.2. Following the principles of Corporate Ethics, influencers and WISY are committed to jointly promoting the brand, building the trust of the community, and creating a positive image of WISY.
